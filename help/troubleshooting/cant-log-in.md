# Can't log in

Troubleshooting steps if you're having trouble logging in to Standout.

## Check your credentials

1. Verify your email address is correct
2. Check for typos in your password
3. Ensure that Caps-Lock is not enabled on your keyboard
4. Try resetting your password

## Password reset

1. Go to the [forgot password page](https://app.integrationer.se/users/password/new)
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Still having issues?

- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check if your account is active
- Contact support if problems persist
