Standout provides quick ways to find what you need and move between different areas.

## Using search

Press the **"s"** key anywhere to open the search field.

- Search works in real-time as you type
- Results appear automatically
- Click a result or press Enter to open it
- Navigate with Arrow Up and Arrow Down keys
- Press Escape to close the search

You can search for:

- Organizations by name
- Integrations by name

## Side menu navigation

Use the side menu to move between main areas:

- **Organizations**: List of all organizations
- **Integrations**: Your workflow integrations
- **Accounts**: Connections to service accounts
- **Members**: Team members in the organization
- **Billing**: Plans, subscriptions, and invoices
- **Activity log**: Log of changes and events

## Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs show your current location in Standout. They appear at the top of the page.

- Each breadcrumb is a clickable link
- Click any breadcrumb to go back to that section
- Breadcrumbs help you understand where you are
- They make it easy to navigate back to previous levels

For example, if you are viewing a specific integration, breadcrumbs might show: **[Organization Name] > Integrations > [Integration Name]**

## Quick navigation tips

- Use the search for quick access to specific items
- The side menu shows your current location
- Click the organization name to switch organizations
- Use browser back/forward buttons to navigate history
