# Creating your user account

Learn how to create your user account and get started.

## Steps to create your account

1. Go to [app.integrationer.se](https://app.integrationer.se)
2. Click on "Sign up"
3. Fill in your details
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile setup

## What's next?

After creating your account, you can:

- Invite teammates to your organization
- Set up your first integration
- Explore your organizations and integrations
