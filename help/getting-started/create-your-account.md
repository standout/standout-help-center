Get started with Standout by creating your account. Follow these simple steps to set up your account and begin using the platform.

## Step 1: Go to the registration page

[Watch this step in the video](https://loom.com/share/e5825ee88d7e4bc192a3c0426c00b336?t=1)

![Registration page](https://loom.com/i/e70cde9a5e8e4690b31630c0c8d1d7fd?workflows_screenshot=true)

- Visit the [Standout login page](https://app.integrationer.se/users/sign_in).
- Click the button to register a new account.

## Step 2: Fill in the registration form

[Watch this step in the video](https://loom.com/share/e5825ee88d7e4bc192a3c0426c00b336?t=19)

![Registration form](https://loom.com/i/f34fd8e453f64284af2cd87527ad9b92?workflows_screenshot=true)

- Enter your email address.
- Type in your name.
- Choose a password.
- Accept the terms and conditions.

## Step 3: Verify your email address

[Watch this step in the video](https://loom.com/share/e5825ee88d7e4bc192a3c0426c00b336?t=51)

![Email verification](https://loom.com/i/e3e14d0342154a37983dce2ecdef52bd?workflows_screenshot=true)

- You will receive a verification code sent to your email.
- You can copy the code or click the link in the email message.

## Step 4: Enter the verification code

[Watch this step in the video](https://loom.com/share/e5825ee88d7e4bc192a3c0426c00b336?t=67)

![Enter verification code](https://loom.com/i/d4ef04f66ebc4ea49b73d32a8853db65?workflows_screenshot=true)

- Paste the verification code on the registration page.
- You can also type the code manually.

## Step 5: Create your organization

[Watch this step in the video](https://loom.com/share/e5825ee88d7e4bc192a3c0426c00b336?t=76)

![Create organization](https://loom.com/i/482baea5de9c43cfa9dd536d017d3bd6?workflows_screenshot=true)

- Enter your company name to create your organization.
- You are now registered and can start using the platform.
